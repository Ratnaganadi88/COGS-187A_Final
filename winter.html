Winter Fest

When: Early winter quarter

Where: Sixth College

Hosted by: Sixth College Technology Committee

For whom: All students

What it is about:

Winter GameFest is an annual electronic sports event, where competition in virtual environments brings members of the video-game community together in a celebration of gaming culture and tradition

Martin Luther King Jr. Day Parade

When: January (Saturday before Martin Luther King Jr. Day)

Where: Downtown San Diego

Hosted by: Center for Student Involvement

For whom: Students, staff, and faculty

What it is about:

Students, staff, and faculty join to march and represent the UCSD Principles of Community. After the parade, join your friends and colleagues for a day of community service honoring Dr. King's ideals. Sign up with the Center for Student Involvement to participate.

Engineering Week

When: Mid-February

Where: Across campus

Hosted by: Engineering students and supporters

For whom: All students

What it is about:

Several days of events, including social activities, an outreach program for local middle-school students, research lab expo, engineering competitions, and career-planning workshops

Dr. Seuss's Birthday Party

When: March 2 (or nearest weekday)

Where: Courtyard in front of Geisel Library entrance

Hosted by: UCSD Libraries

For whom: All students

What it is about:

Celebrate the birthday of author, illustrator, and UCSD benefactor Theodor Seuss Geisel with music, storytelling, a giant Cat in the Hat balloon, and noontime punch and cake for all partygoers. Be sure to check out the library's Dr. Seuss Collection while you're there
