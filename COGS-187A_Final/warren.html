High Table Dinners

When: Each quarter

Where: Locations vary by year

Hosted by: Warren College Student Council

For whom: Professors and warren college students

What it is about:

Each quarter the Provost hosts a High Table Dinner designed to enrich faculty-student relationships. This evening of fine food and conversation for a small number of students and faculty is loosely based on the Oxford and Cambridge tradition, where college faculty and students dine together in a formal and intimate setting. Space is limited and advance registration is required.

Warren Live!

When: March

Where: Price Center

Hosted by: Warren College Student Council Concerts and Festivals Commission

For whom: Students from all colleges

What it is about:

This annual Warren College event brings up-and-coming artists to the UCSD stage for the entire campus community to enjoy, and is free to all students.

Orientation Bear Photo

When: September

Where: At the stone bear in warren college

Hosted by:Freshman Orientation Program

For whom: Warren College freshmen

What it is about:

Every year during Fall Orientation new students are photographed with Bear, a piece of the Stuart Art Collection located within Warren College. This photo serves as an official class photo.
