Chocolate Festival

When: spring quarter

Where: vary by events

Hosted by: Sixth College Community Board

For whom: students from all colleges

What it is about:

The Sixth College Chocolate Festival has been a spring quarter tradition since 2004. The event attracts over 600 guests from across the University. The annual festival includes chocolate fountains, lots of sweet and savory dipping items, music, prizes, music, and activities for all ages.

Sixth Digital Film Festival

When: Winter Quarter

Where: Price Center Theater

Hosted by: Sixth College Video Production Club

For whom: Students from all colleges

What it is about:

Coordinated by the Sixth College Video Production Club, this annual contest screens short films by UCSD students, and gives awards in several categories, including "best in fest."
