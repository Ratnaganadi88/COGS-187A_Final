The Global Marketplace

When: Monthly

Where: Cafe Ventanas

For whom: ERC Students

What is it about:

TSince 1993, ERC has celebrated a different region of the world each month (October – Latin America; November – North America; January – Middle East; February – Africa; March – Oceana; April – Europe; May – Asia) with an exciting evening featuring cultural entertainment, crafts, education, and a culturally inspired meal catered by Cafe Ventanas.

SLAM

When: Monthly

Where: Locations vary by events

Hosted by: I-House

What it is about:

"Spontaneous Laughter, Art, and Music" is International House's monthly community open-mic event that began in 2000 and has become a showcase for budding singers, instrumentalists, stand-up comedians, and spoken-word artists from all six colleges.

Intercultural Communication Workshop

When: Winter quarter

Where: Location varies each year

Hosted by: ERC College

For whom: ERC College students

What it is about:

ERC began this 5-session weekly workshop in 1993 as a feature of its programming theme, "A Celebration of Cultures at Home and Abroad." Since then, the workshops have continued to provide all UCSD students with the tools and skills needed to communicate across cultures.

Rock'n'Roosevelt

When: Early spring quarter

Where: Cafe Ventanas

Hosted by: Rock 'n' Roosevelt Committee

For whom: Students from all colleges

What it is about:

A concert on the green in front of Cafe Ventanas, sponsored by the Student Council at ERC.
