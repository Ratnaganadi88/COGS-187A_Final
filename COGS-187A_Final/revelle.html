Holiday Vendor Fair

When: Late November/ early December

Where: Sun God Lawn

Hosted by:Revelle and Muir

For whom: Students for all colleges

What it is about:

Shop for holiday gifts while helping to raise funds for the college's semi-formal dance.

Roger Revelle's birthday celebration

When: March

Where: Revelle Plaza

Hosted by: Revelle Programming Board

For whom: Revelle College Students

What it is about:

Enjoy music, a barbecue lunch, and birthday cake in honor of college founder Roger Revelle.

Revelle College Semiformal

When: March

Where: Location varies by year

Hosted by: Revelle Semi Formal Committee

For whom: Revelle College Students

What it is about:

Dress to impress at Revelle's annual off-campus dance.

Watermelon Drop

When: June

Where: Urey Residential Hall

Hosted by: Revelle College

For whom: Students from all colleges

What it is about:

UCSD's oldest continuing tradition (observed since 1965) marks the end of the school year. The newly elected Watermelon Queen tosses the fruit from the top of 7-story Urey Hall in an attempt to create new velocity/ splat records. The drop is followed by a festival and barbecue with plenty of free watermelon.
