AS Pancake Breakfast

When: Finals week of fall, winter, and spring quarters

Where: Price Center

Hosted by: Associated Students

For whom: All students

What it is about:

The Associated Students make pancakes and all the fixin's to give students a much-needed sugar break while studying for finals

Farmer's Market

When: Tuesdays from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. in fall, winter, and spring quarters

Where: Town Square, in front of the Student Services Center

Hosted by: Center for Student Involvement and the Sunshine Store

For whom: Everyone

What it is about:

Local vendors sell fresh seasonal produce, flowers, and a variety of prepared specialty foods

I-House Language Tables

When: Weekly (various times)

Where: Cafe Ventanas

Hosted by: Eleanor Roosevelt College's International House

For whom: Students, faculty, staff, and community members

What it is about:

International House residents host informal discussions in their native languages with other students, faculty, staff, and community members who are interested in practicing their foreign language skills

I-House Culture Nights

When: Once each quarter

Where: International House

Hosted by: Eleanor Roosevelt College Residence Life Office

For whom: All students

What it is about:

Since its inception in 1988, International House has hosted a series of quarterly events highlighting the cultures that make up the I-House community: European Culture Night (fall quarter), Americas Culture Night (winter), and Asian-Pacific Culture Night (spring). I-House residents create an evening-long program by cooking a meal and producing entertainment events.

TGIO

When: Friday of Week 10 in fall, winter, and spring quarters

Where: Price Center

Hosted by: Associated Students

For whom: All students

What it is about:

A concert to celebrate the end of the quarter.
