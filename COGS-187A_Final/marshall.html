Marshall Spirit Crew Tailgaters

When: February

Where: Location varies every year

Hosted by: Marshall Spirit Crew

For whom: Thurgood Marshall Students

What it is about:

MSC gets everybody pumped for UCSD sports. Face painting, sign making, and food eating: all you need to better cheer on Triton athletes!

California Indigenous People’s Day

When: 4th Friday in September (Fall Quarter)

Where: International Center

What it is about:

Celebrate the heritage and culture of California's Native Peoples with the Kick-Off event at the UC San Diego International Center. More information to come.

Where: Loactions vary by events

Hosted by: MarshallPalooza Committee

Marshallpalooza is the one of the biggest highlights of Fall Quarter, and is the free student-run carnival and concert of the year.
